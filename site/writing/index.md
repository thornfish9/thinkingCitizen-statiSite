Welcome to **The Thinking Citizen**.

This site is a collection of practical guides, systems thinking tools, and explorations of how complex systems actually work.

The goal is simple: help thoughtful people understand the world more clearly and act more effectively within it.

## Sections

- **Guides** – Practical how-to material and deep dives.
- **Infrastructure** – Notes on building robust systems.
- **Tools** – Thinking tools, diagrams, and frameworks.
- **Projects** – Ongoing experiments and builds.
